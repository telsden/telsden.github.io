Hi! I'm a Post Doctoral Research Fellow funded by the Leverhulme trust, working with Dr Andrew Wright , in the Solar and Magnetospheric Theory group within the School of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of St Andrews, Scotland. My research involves the study of ultra low frequency (ULF) waves in Earth's magnetosphere. We primarily consider the numerical modelling of ULF waves and attempt to match results from our models to observations. In this way, we hope to shed more light on the observations and explore some of the basic features of ULF waves such as:

  • Field line resonance and wave coupling.
  • Magnetopause driven ULF waves.
  • Energy propagation within the outer magnetosphere.
