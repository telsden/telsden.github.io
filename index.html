Hello! I am a Leverhulme Early Career Fellow at the University of Glasgow, in the School of Mathematics and Statistics. My research involves the study of ultra low frequency (ULF) waves in Earth's magnetosphere. I primarily consider the numerical modelling of ULF waves using the framework of magnetohydrodynamics (MHD) and attempt to match results from models to observations.
